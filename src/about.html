Welcome to Our Website

This project aims to provide a seamless experience for users to access information through QR codes. By scanning the QR codes, users can be redirected to specific pages containing relevant information.

Our website is designed to be user-friendly and accessible, ensuring that everyone can benefit from the information provided.

Features

  • Easy navigation through the website
  • Responsive design for all devices
  • Quick access to information via QR codes
